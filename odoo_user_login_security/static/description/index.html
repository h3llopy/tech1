Secure your Odoo from Intruders with User Login Security

From ERP to E-commerce, Odoo helps you to manage your business completely. Hence, it encloses important data of a company.

Have you ever thought about the loss to businesses caused due to data theft?

Cyber-security Ventures predicted that cyber-crime will cost the world $6 trillion annually by 2021.

Data theft is mostly associated with the theft of bank details. Not only this, but the data theft of a business can lead to several serious problems. This includes theft of client information, identity theft, theft of intellectual property, sabotage, resource theft, and many more.

Here comes the role of a secure login system to your Odoo Account

Your Odoo Account Login Security Problems has got one solution. User Login Security module presents abundant features to secure your Odoo login activity.

Secure your Odoo account from Brute-force attack

Allowing multiple login attempts is one of the biggest threats. It's call of the time to safeguard your Odoo account from Brute-force attack.

User login security allows you to easily configure the limit of Failed Login Attempts.

What's more!

You can also set the lockout time; once the user will reach the maximum limit of failed login attempts.

Reduce the Risk of Cyber Attacks

Preventing multiple login sessions at a time can reduce the risk of account hacking to a great extent.

As it directly stops attacker to get into your account if you are already logged in. Accomplishing the same for your Odoo account is feasible with User Login Security in Odoo.

Do you have a habit of Leaving System without Logging Out?

It is really important to log out of your account once you leave your system. If not than anyone else can misuse it. The module facilitates you to set the session expiry time limit.

Hence, your Odoo Account will be logged Out automatically after a preset time of inactivity. You can also set the background actions which you don't want to consider in the activeness of the session.
